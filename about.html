About

Founded by Gabriel Garayalde at the start of 2023, StructOptima was born with the goal of providing intuitive, easy to use web applications that can give the most value added to every practicing engineer without the need for expensive licenses and complicated software.

Gabriel has a masters degree in structural engineering with a focus on the intersection between artificial intelligence and optimization of structures. He has worked in the industry for 5 years and has experience with a range of different projects from residential to commercial to industrial.
