Column Design to AS4100

The following program yields the optimal member size for a simply supported beam column under combined axial compression and major axis bending. The program takes as input the values of the slider and calculates the most efficient member size for in-plane Major axis bending. It assumes that the member is fully laterally restrained.

Failure Modes

9 Checks are carried out for the beam-column member under compression and applied Moments. The score is a measure of the capacity of utilization of the load with respect to the capacity.

Failure Mode

Score Calculation

1(a). Member Compression Capacity Ncx

$$ = \frac{N^*}{\phi N_cx}$$

1(b). Member Compression Capacity Ncy

$$ = \frac{N^*}{\phi N_cy}$$

2. Member Moment Capacity Mbx

$$ = \frac{M_x^*}{\phi M_{bx}}$$

3. Section Moment Capacity Msy

$$ = \frac{M_y^*}{\phi M_{sy}}$$

4(a). Reduced Section Moment Capacity Mrx

$$ = \frac{M_x^*}{\phi M_{rx}}$$

4(b). Reduced Section Moment Capacity Mry

$$ = \frac{M_y^*}{\phi M_{ry}}$$

5(a). In-plane Member Moment Capacity Mix

$$ = \frac{M_x^*}{\phi M_{ix}}$$

5(b). In-plane Member Moment Capacity Miy

$$ = \frac{M_y^*}{\phi M_{iy}}$$

6. Member Biaxial Bending Capacity

$$ = [\frac{M_x^*}{\phi M_{cx}}]^{1.4} + [\frac{M_y^*}{\phi M_{iy}}]^{1.4} $$
